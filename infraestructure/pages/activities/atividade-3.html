Introduction

Monkey D. Luffy is the main character of One Piece, a globally renowned anime and manga created by Eiichiro Oda. Luffy is a fun-loving, fearless, and determined pirate who dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates by finding the legendary treasure known as the One Piece, hidden at the end of the Grand Line.

Key Facts About Luffy

  • Full Name: Monkey D. Luffy
  • Nickname: Straw Hat Luffy
  • Birthday: May 5
  • Bounty: 3,000,000,000 Berries
  • Devil Fruit: Gomu Gomu no Mi (later Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika)
  • Haki: Observation, Armament, Conqueror’s
  • Affiliation: Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates

The Straw Hat Pirates

  • Roronoa Zoro – Swordsman aiming to become the best in the world.
  • Nami – Navigator dreaming of mapping the entire world.
  • Usopp – Sniper who wants to be a brave warrior of the sea.
  • Sanji – Cook searching for the mythical All Blue.
  • Tony Tony Chopper – Reindeer doctor aiming to cure all diseases.
  • Nico Robin – Archaeologist who seeks the true history.
  • Franky – Cyborg shipwright who built the Thousand Sunny.
  • Brook – Musician with the Revive-Revive Fruit.
  • Jinbe – Helmsman and Fish-Man Karate master.

Allies and Rivals

  • Shanks – Yonko who inspired Luffy
  • Trafalgar D. Water Law – Captain of the Heart Pirates
  • Sabo – Luffy’s sworn brother and Revolutionary
  • Boa Hancock – Pirate Empress in love with Luffy
  • Yamato – Kaido’s child, ally of Luffy
  • Enemies: Blackbeard, Akainu, Kaido, Big Mom
